    Spooky Eyes

    Having fun with an Arduino to create spooky eyes for a bithday party or Halloween. 2x 10mm diffused red LEDs are used for the eyes and they are controlled by an Arduino Mini.

    I have an iron helmet in which I'm gonna place the eyes. The eyes will do the following:

    • open (fade in)
    • close (fade out)
    • when open, they blink every 10-15 seconds
    • they can go crazy (flicker)

    The arduino will be powered by a 9V battery.
